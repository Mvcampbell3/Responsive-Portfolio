About Me

Welcome to my Website! I am a very motivated and hardworking student of web design. I am looking forward to being able to create internet destinations that really grab people's attention. I also greatly enjoy the technical side of web design. Coding programs and applications that are useful for many people will be very rewarding.

My past experience in various fields will be very helpful for me moving forward into this field. I have tons of experience in team-oriented environments due to my time both playing baseball at the college and professional level, and coaching at the highschool level. I have a lot of experience working in the customer satisfaction market, mainly the service industry. Getting instructions and turning them into finished products will not be a new skill for me.

Finally, my personal life is a great source of motivation and happiness for me. I have a four year old son. He is a crazy ball of energy who needs someone he can look up to and respect. I am very motivated to be that person for him. This path that I am on is an exciting one, a challenging one, and one I am unwilling to let pass without my full efforts to succeed.
